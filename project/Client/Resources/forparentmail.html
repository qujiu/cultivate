FAQ

1, No sounds while you are using the apps?

First of all, please make sure that you switched sounds (device) on, then check sound setting in relative app. The volume could be adjusted optionally.

2, Not able to save the image?

Click “camera” which shows on the screen, then choose image to be saved into your device. Please allow app to access your album.

If this tips doesn't fix the problem, please following these steps;

Open your device, click “Setting”→ “Privacy” →“Albums”, then find apps and tap the “open” button .

3, What if you accidentally delete your apps after purchasing?

Please don`t worry!

Once you purchase our apps successfully, kindly notice that the relative transaction records were recorded in our system automatically. So when you delete an app by accident, please don`t worry about the system will charge you twice. All you need to do is just find that app, click “Buy” button, re-download it as usual, then then it can be used again, and you will not be charged a second time.

If your account logged onto a different devices, and the purchased goods or roles are still state as “unlock”. Please click the “Buy” button,then the relative items will be unlocked for free. Or click the “Shop” button on home page, then choose Re-Buy to unlock the objects.
